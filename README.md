                                                      Abstract

Gait recognition can be used in person identification and re-identification by itself or in conjunction with other biometrics. Although gait has both spatial and temporal attributes, and it has been observed that decoupling spatial feature and temporal feature can better exploit the gait feature on the finegrained level. However, the spatial-temporal correlations of gait video signals are also lost in the decoupling process. Direct 3D convolution approaches can retain such correlations, but they also introduce unnecessary interferences. Instead of common 3D convolution solutions, this paper proposes an integration of decoupling process into a 3D convolution framework for crossview gait recognition. In particular, a novel block consisting of a Parallel-insight Convolution layer integrated with a SpatialTemporal Dual-Attention (STDA) unit is proposed as the basic block for global spatial-temporal information extraction. Under the guidance of the STDA unit, this block can well integrate spatial-temporal information extracted by two decoupled models and at the same time retain the spatial-temporal correlations. In addition, a Multi-Scale Salient Feature Extractor is proposed to further exploit the fine-grained features through context awareness extension of part-based features and adaptively aggregating the spatial features. Extensive experiments on three popular gait datasets, namely CASIA-B, OULP and OUMVLP, demonstrate that the proposed method outperforms state-of-theart methods.

                                                        摘要

步态识别可独立或结合其他生物特征实现人员识别与重识别，其兼具空间和时间属性，解耦空时特征虽能细粒度挖掘步态特征，却会丢失步态视频信号的时空关联性；直接3D卷积虽能保留该关联性，却易引入无关干扰。为此，本文提出将解耦过程融入3D卷积框架的跨视角步态识别方法，设计了融合平行视角卷积层（Pi-Conv）与时空双注意力单元（STDA）的新型模块，作为全局时空信息提取的基础模块，该模块能在STDA单元引导下，有效融合两个解耦模型提取的时空信息并保留时空关联性。同时，本文提出多尺度显著特征提取器（MSSFE），通过基于部件特征的上下文感知扩展和空间特征的自适应聚合，进一步挖掘细粒度步态特征。在CASIA-B、OULP和OUMVLP三个主流步态数据集上的大量实验表明，所提方法性能优于当前主流的跨视角步态识别方法。本文的核心贡献为三方面：其一，设计Pi-Conv层，实现直接3D卷积与解耦建模的协同融合；其二，提出适用于基于轮廓序列的步态识别的STDA单元，自适应调整Pi-Conv层输出，提升空时解耦建模与直接3D卷积的融合效果；其三，构建MSSFE，扩展各部件的上下文感知范围并自适应聚合空间特征，更高效地实现鲁棒的基于部件的特征学习。
